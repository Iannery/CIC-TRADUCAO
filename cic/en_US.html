  • News

    UnB students in the Medal Table of the Brazilian Computer Science Olympiad (OBI 2022)

    Gold Medal: Eduardo Quirino de Oliveira (CIC); Gold Medal: Wilson Oliveira Guimarães Neto (CIC); Bronze Medal: Lucas Sala Cruz (CIC); Honorable Mention: Henrique de Oliveira Ramos (CIC); Honorable Mention: Iago Rocha Santos Marques (UnB-Gama).   Among 1,883 participants in OBI 2022 in the Programming Mode Senior Level, 4 students from the Computer Science Computer Science Department (CIC) from UnB-Darcy Ribeiro won positions in the Medal Table, with 2 Gold Medals, 1 Bronze Medal and 1 Honorable Mention. A student from UnB-Gama also received an Honorable Mention. OBI is a competition organized along the lines of the other Brazilian scientific olympics, with the objective of arousing in the students the interest for computer science through an activity that involves challenge, ingenuity and a great deal of imagination.   Source: https://olimpiada.ic.unicamp.br/resultados/qmerito/ps/.

    Outstanding result in the IEEExtreme international programming competition

    Our 5 teams ranked globally 18, 46, 577, 588 and 718, out of 3522 teams! Within Brazil, we got first, second, fourth, fifth, and sixth places! Congratulations to all participants for this exceptional result!      The IEEExtreme 2022 - 24-hour programming competition (https://ieeextreme.org/) took place from Friday (10/21) at 9pm to Saturday (10/22) at 9pm. Every hour or two, one or two problems were made available. In all there were 25 problems.       There were 3522 teams competing and UnB had 5 teams. Our students performed exceptionally well!!!       Two teams from UnB made the top 100: RockLeeNaoMandaWA and Flamengo. The RockLeeNaoMandaWA team (Alberto Neto, Leonardo Riether, Tiago Fernandes) placed 18th, first in Brazil and second in Latin America! Team Flamengo (Enzo Niho, Eduardo Quirino de Oliveira, Wilson Guimaraes) placed 46th, second place in Brazil!      The other three teams - sintomuitoporvoce (Victor Santos, Nathalia Pereira), TombadospeloGetLine (Bruno de Souza, Pedro de Oliveira, Gustavo Starling) and NuncaMaisEuVouDormir (Victor Hugo Lisboa, Lucas Cruz, Henrique Ramos) - ranked 577, 588 and 718, respectively, placing 4, 5 and 6 in Brazil!      Congratulations to the competitors for the exciting competition! The students make us proud of being Brazilians and of being part of the CIC/UnB!
